Plan, Train & Race Smarter with PaceWise

From intervals to race strategy—PaceWise helps you train with purpose.

Plan any run in seconds

Quickly calculate your pace per mile or kilometer by entering your distance and time. Whether you're training for a 5K or a marathon, get instant results—including treadmill speed at a 1% incline.

Know your finish time before you run

Choose a distance, set your goal pace, and let PaceWise calculate your total time. Perfect for setting targets or reverse-engineering race day goals.

Visualize your splits—your way

See exactly how your effort breaks down with splits by mile, kilometer, 5K or 5-mile segments. Ideal for long runs, workouts, or race planning.

Nail your intervals with confidence

Get precise pacing for structured workouts—200m to 2000m intervals—based on your target time. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

Estimate race results across distances

Use your performance at one distance to predict results at another. Great for planning tune-up races or checking fitness without overtraining.

Benchmark with age grading

See how your time stacks up across age groups with WMA-based age grading. A simple way to measure performance and progress.

Save & Share Your Workouts

Save your pace calculations and splits to revisit later—or share them with a friend, coach, or training partner. It's easy to export your workout plan by text, email, or anywhere you need it.
