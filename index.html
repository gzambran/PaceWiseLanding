Plan any run in seconds

Set your goal pace, choose a distance, and let PaceWise calculate your total time. Perfect for setting targets or reverse-engineering race day goals.

Get precise pacing for structured workouts—200m to 2000m intervals—based on your target time. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.
