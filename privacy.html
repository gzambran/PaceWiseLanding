Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2025

PaceWise ("we," "our," or "the App") is a running calculator designed to assist users in calculating running paces, splits, and training intervals. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

PaceWise does not collect any personally identifiable information or track any activity related to its users. The app operates entirely offline, and no personal data is transmitted to any third-party services.

2. Saved Data

When you use the app, you have the option to save your calculations. By default, these calculations are saved with a name based on the distance and pace. You may delete your saved calculations at any time directly from the app on your device. These saved calculations are stored locally on your device and are not shared or transmitted to any external servers.

3. No User Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any other tracking technologies to monitor or collect your personal data. We do not track your usage patterns, nor do we gather any other data related to your device or location.

4. Data Security

Since PaceWise does not collect or transmit any personal data, there are no security risks related to data storage or sharing within the app. Your saved calculations are only accessible on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

PaceWise does not use third-party services that collect or process user data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
